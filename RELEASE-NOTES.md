# Release Notes — Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose & Bharat

A free, good-faith fan tribute to Netaji and India's independence struggle.
Newest updates first.

---

## Update 3 — 2026-07-13 · Diplomacy & UI polish

- **Diplomacy portrait redone.** Netaji is now a three-quarter-length painting
  that turns to face the leader he's meeting — matching how the other leaders
  present themselves — instead of a forward-facing bust.
- **Fixed the placeholder figure** that could appear behind Netaji during a
  first meeting (the generic fallback model is now fully covered).
- **Fixed the portrait's framing** — his head is no longer clipped at the top,
  and his scale now matches the leader across from him.
- **Bharat's Ashoka Chakra emblem** now appears on the in-game leader banners
  and on the rankings/score comparison screen (previously blank for Bharat).

## Update 2 — 2026-07-13 · Critical load fix

- **Fixed the mod failing to load.** Enabling the mod could throw
  "Content Configuration Validation Failed" and block the game from starting,
  outside of the Modern Age — affecting everyone. Bharat's unique civics-tree
  data now loads at all times, so the mod is safe to enable from the main menu
  and any age.

## Update 1 — 2026-07-13 · Initial release

- **Leader — Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose** (Militaristic + Diplomatic) with the
  Azad Hind Fauj ability and the Purna Swaraj agenda.
- **Civilization — Bharat** (Modern Age) with the Mother Industries ability,
  four unique units (Azad Hind Fauj, Rani of Jhansi Regiment, Azad Hind Nau
  Sena, Bahadur Group), the National Planning Commission unique quarter, a
  five-node civics tree (Swadeshi → Purna Swaraj), custom art, Ashoka Chakra
  iconography and saffron/navy player colours.
- Portrayed entirely through the independence struggle. Unofficial fan mod; not
  affiliated with Firaxis or 2K. The voice/theme in the intro video are a
  synthesised tribute rendition, not authentic recordings.
