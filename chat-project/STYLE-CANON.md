# Style canon — ratified decisions (grows over time)

The living record of what has been **approved**, so the project improves from
experience instead of relitigating settled choices. CONSULT this before
writing. Where it conflicts with older phrasing in other knowledge files, this
wins. Only the user ratifies entries — Chat *proposes* additions at the end of
a deliverable; nothing is canon until the user says so.

## Locked names & terms
- Civ name: **Bharat** (the independent nation-state; never "India" as the civ label).
- Civ full name: **Republic of Bharat**; adjective: **Bharatiya**. *(ratified 2026-07-12)*
- **Azad Hind Radio** (Bose's broadcasts) — never "Tokyo Radio" (framing rule). *(Now a leader-ability effect — the memento was dropped, see Rejected.)*
- Leader ability: **Azad Hind Fauj**. Agenda: **Purna Swaraj**.
- Greeting/slogans in play: "Jai Hind", "Dilli Chalo", "Give me blood, and I shall give you freedom".
- **"Jai Hind!"** is the standard closing salute for Bharat civ text. *(ratified 2026-07-12)*
- The **unique unit shares the name "Azad Hind Fauj"** with the leader ability — intentional (both *are* the army); never disambiguate them. *(ratified 2026-07-12)*
- Unique quarter **National Planning Commission** = buildings **Yojana Bhavan** (planned industry) + **Vaidyashala** (planned public health, Ayurveda/Sushruta heritage). *(ratified 2026-07-12)*
- Unique civics (5): **Swadeshi, Purna Swaraj, Forward Bloc, Azad Hind, National Planning**. *(ratified 2026-07-12)*
- **`{X}`/`{Y}`/`{Z}` placeholders** are the convention for Code-slotted numbers in draft functional text. *(ratified 2026-07-12)*

## Approved voice exemplars
- **Ancient-heritage as inheritance, never as bonus** *(ratified 2026-07-12)*:
  invoke India's ancient science as figures in a lineage — "its astronomers,
  its surgeons, its masters of steel" (Aryabhata, Sushruta, Wootz) — that a
  free people *inherit*, never as literal gameplay effects. Exemplar: the
  Bharat loading intro ("The Maurya carved their edicts in stone; the Chola
  sent their bronzes across the southern seas… Bharat rises… Jai Hind!").
- **Civ text closes on the salute, not the leader's imagery**: Bharat civ text
  ends on "Jai Hind!" rather than echoing the leader intro's red-fort /
  blood-for-freedom lines (keep leader and civ voices distinct).
- **Leader and unit combat bonuses mirror each other** *(ratified 2026-07-12)*:
  the leader defends the homeland (+CS *inside* friendly territory), the Azad
  Hind Fauj marches to liberate (+CS *outside* it) — complementary, not
  overlapping.

## Historical record (corrections that must hold)
- The **1938 National Planning Committee**: Bose **convened** it as Congress
  president; **Nehru chaired** it. Always "convened", never "chaired".
  *(corrected 2026-07-12 — the Chat project caught this in DESIGN/brief.)*

## Rejected approaches (do not revisit)
- Vimana / Vaimanika Shastra as a mechanic or flavor — rejected (pseudoscience; Workshop liability).
- Any Axis-alliance reference, mechanic, or iconography — rejected (framing rule).
- Religious-identity framing for Bharat — rejected in favor of composite nationalism + civilizational knowledge.
- Memento power above official minor-memento magnitude (e.g. +2 combat) — rejected; use +1.
- **Custom (mod-added) mementos entirely** — rejected: they can't be made
  equippable without fragile core-UI-file replacement. The two we built
  (Springing Tiger, Azad Hind Radio) were dropped and their effects refolded
  into the leader ability. Don't re-attempt. *(2026-07-12)*

## Visual identity (Chat-project ruling 2026-07-12 — PENDING user ratification of the two "teeth" items)

- **Flag lineage (research foundation):** the 1931 Karachi Congress tricolour is the
  common ancestor of both flags — Azad Hind flew it with the springing tiger
  (Port Blair, 30 Dec 1943); the Republic adopted it with the Ashoka Chakra
  (22 Jul 1947, chakra = the Sarnath dharmachakra, Mauryan). The republic's
  symbols are literally Mauryan artifacts — the Maurya → Chola → Bharat thread
  made visible.
- **Iconography split:** LEADER/INA subjects (leader portrait, leader loading
  intro, unit flag) use ONLY Azad Hind symbols — Springing Tiger + Azad Hind
  tricolour. CIVILIZATION/state subjects (civ symbol, civic-tree badge,
  quarter/building icons, civ loading still, player colors, Workshop banner)
  may use Republic symbols — chakra tricolour + Sarnath Lion Capital. The civ
  IS the republic; the leader is its herald.
- **Non-sectarian basis (explicit):** the Ashoka Chakra and Lion Capital are
  state/civilizational symbols — Mauryan heritage chosen by the Constituent
  Assembly for its trans-communal character — never sectarian marks.
- **Timeline rule:** the chakra flag (1947) and State Emblem (1950) postdate
  Bose's death (Aug 1945). Never depict either as a period prop in any scene
  of Bose or the INA — never in his hands. Symbolic composites (Workshop
  banner) may set republic emblems BEHIND him as emblematic background.
- **[TEETH #1 — user to ratify] Civ symbol:** 24-spoke Ashoka Chakra replaces
  the Springing Tiger as the Bharat civ symbol; civic-tree badge reuses the
  same chakra mark. The tiger remains the leader-side mark and the Azad Hind
  Fauj unit flag.
- **Lion Capital placements:** National Planning Commission quarter icon +
  Bharat loading still. Always the ancient Sarnath sculpture (lions, abacus,
  lotus bell) — NEVER the official State Emblem lockup with "Satyameva Jayate"
  (State Emblem of India Act 2005; Workshop liability — same logic as the
  Vimana rejection).
- **Devanagari is ART-ONLY** (game ships no Devanagari font — verified).
  Approved strings: भारत ("Bharat"), जय हिन्द ("Jai Hind"). Approved placements:
  loading-still title treatment, Workshop banner. Never in icons, never in
  engine-rendered text. Civ display name stays Latin "Bharat" (historical
  cover: the INA's official medium was Hindustani in Roman script, chosen to
  be script-neutral across its Hindu-Muslim-Sikh ranks — Fay 1993).
- **Devanagari production rule:** never let an image model generate glyphs;
  reserve negative space and composite real typography (Noto Serif Devanagari /
  macOS Devanagari fonts) over the art in post.
- **[TEETH #2 — user to ratify] Player colors** are flag-derived only,
  including chakra navy #000080. Default: saffron field #FF9933 / navy symbol
  #000080 (~7.5:1 contrast). Alts: green/white, white/navy, navy/saffron.
  Saffron-on-green pairing REJECTED (~2:1 contrast, unreadable at zoom).
- **Production note (chakra):** image models cannot count 24 spokes — draw the
  final chakra geometry programmatically; use any generation only as texture
  reference.
