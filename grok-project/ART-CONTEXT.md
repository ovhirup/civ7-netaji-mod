## The subject in one paragraph

Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose: Indian independence leader, founder-commander of the
Indian National Army (Azad Hind Fauj). Iconic look — **round spectacles**, INA
**field uniform with peaked cap**, or a dark **sherwani**. His emblem is the
**Springing Tiger**; his colours the **Azad Hind tricolour** (saffron/white/green
with a leaping tiger). Bharat the civilization = a modern, industrialising,
composite-nationalist India: planned industry, unity of many peoples,
self-reliance. Dignified, heroic, historical. No Axis imagery, no religious
iconography, no traced photographs. (See custom instructions for the full rule.)

## Global look

Painterly, warm, slightly stylized realism in the style of Civilization VII's
own leader portraits and civ emblems. One consistent hand across all assets.
Suggested prompt seed to adapt per asset:
> "Painterly stylized-realistic [SUBJECT], in the visual style of a Sid Meier's
> Civilization VII [asset type], warm dramatic lighting, [palette], centered
> composition, [flat solid background / dark neutral backdrop], no text."

## Assets (priority order)

### 1. Civ emblem — the Springing Tiger  ★ do first
A single, instantly-readable **monochrome silhouette** of the INA Springing
Tiger — a leaping/springing tiger, side profile, bold and clean, the kind of
glyph that still reads at 32 px. Flat, centred, symmetrical-ish. Generate as a
**solid white or solid black tiger on the opposite flat solid colour** (it will
be flattened to pure white on transparent). No gradients, no scenery, no text.

### 2. Building icon — Yojana Bhavan (planning ministry)  ★ do first
A painterly building/emblem icon (~square) reading at menu size. Theme: **planned
industry / the Planning Commission** — a modernist ministry building, or a
stylized motif of a factory + blueprint + five-year-plan chart, or a dam/turbine.
Warm industrial palette (steel, ochre, saffron accents). Flat solid background.

### 3. Building icon — Vaidyashala (house of healing)  ★ do first
A painterly building/emblem icon. Theme: **traditional Indian medicine / a house
of healing** — mortar and pestle, medicinal herbs, stylized Sushruta-era
instruments, an Ayurvedic clinic. Calm green + ochre palette (health, growth).
Flat solid background. Must feel a sibling of the Yojana Bhavan icon (same hand).

### 4. Civic-tree node icon — `cult_bharat`
A small roundel/emblem used on the 5 civic-tree nodes (currently a magenta
placeholder in game). Suggested: the **tricolour + Springing Tiger roundel**, or
a simplified Springing Tiger badge. Bold, simple, reads tiny. Flat solid
background. Can share DNA with the civ emblem but distinct enough to sit on a
node card.

### 5. Leader portrait — Netaji  (confirm display, then finalize)
A **painted bust portrait**: three-quarter view, dignified and resolute, calm
determination. Wardrobe: **INA field uniform with peaked cap** (the iconic look)
or a dark sherwani; **round spectacles are essential** to recognizability. Warm,
dramatic lighting; **dark neutral painted background** (the game crops to a
hexagon and a circle, so keep head + shoulders centred with margin). Original
painted likeness — never trace a photo. Deliver large; several angles/wardrobe
variants welcome so the best can be chosen.

### 6. Unit flag — Azad Hind Fauj
A small, high-contrast **unit-flag emblem** in the game's flag style (reads on
the map at small size). Subject: the **Springing Tiger** insignia, or crossed
rifles beneath the tiger. Bolder/simpler than the civ emblem. Flat solid
background.

### 7. (Optional, later) National Planning Commission — quarter icon
Composite motif combining the industry (Yojana Bhavan) + health (Vaidyashala)
themes; or simply reuse the Yojana Bhavan icon if a distinct one isn't needed.

### 8. (Later) Loading-screen background
A wide painterly scene — the INA on the march under the Azad Hind tricolour,
"Dilli Chalo" energy, dawn light. Cinematic, ~16:9. This one keeps its full
scene (no keying), so a rich background is good here.

### 9. (Later) Workshop banner
Marketing image for the mod page: Netaji + a "Bharat" title treatment, the
tricolour and Springing Tiger. Any wide size.
